**🎤 J.A.R.V.I.S. - Voice Assistant**

*A sleek, voice-controlled AI assistant inspired by Iron Man’s J.A.R.V.I.S. 🔥*  

---

## **✨ Features**

✅ **Voice Commands** – "Play music", "Open YouTube", "Tell me a joke"  
✅ **Smart Music Player** – Finds songs even with mispronounced names  
✅ **Web Automation** – Opens Google, YouTube, Facebook, ChatGPT  
✅ **Time & Date** – Speaks current time/date  
✅ **Jokes & Responses** – Interactive and fun  

---

## **⚙️ Tech Stack**

-> **speech_recognition** → Listens to your voice  
-> **pyttsx3** → Talks back (customizable voice)  
-> **webbrowser** → Opens websites instantly  
-> **difflib** → Fuzzy-matches song requests  
-> **datetime** → Keeps track of time  

---

## **🎵 Music Library**
  
- Stores YouTube links (expandable!)  
- Example: Say *"Play Bikhra"* → Opens correct song  

---

## **🚀 Quick Start**

1. Run `main.py`  
2. Say *"Jarvis"* to activate  
3. Give commands like:  
   - *"Open Google"*  
   - *"Play Tu Hai Kahaan"*  
   - *"What’s the time?"*  

---
